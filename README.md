# MarketGazer 📈✨

MarketGazer is a cutting-edge, AI-powered stock market analysis and virtual portfolio tracking application. Designed for modern investors, it combines real-time data visualization with advanced generative AI to provide deep market insights.

## 🚀 Key Features

- **Intelligence Dashboard**: A high-level overview of your portfolio performance, current market sentiment (Bullish/Bearish), and top market movers.
- **AI Price Prediction**: Leverage Genkit-powered AI models to forecast future stock prices based on historical trends and market data.
- **Personalized Recommendations**: Get tailored stock suggestions based on your existing portfolio holdings and specific risk tolerance.
- **Advanced Risk Assessment**: Instantly analyze any stock ticker for potential risks, incorporating market volatility and real-time news sentiment.
- **Portfolio Management**: Track your virtual holdings with precision, monitoring daily gains, losses, and total asset value.
- **Global Market News**: Stay informed with a curated feed of financial news aggregated from top global sources.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **Framework**: [Next.js 15](https://nextjs.org/) (App Router)
- **UI Library**: [React 18](https://reactjs.org/) with [ShadCN UI](https://ui.shadcn.com/)
- **Styling**: [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com/)
- **AI Engine**: [Genkit](https://firebase.google.com/docs/genkit) (Gemini 2.5 Flash)
- **Backend & Auth**: [Firebase](https://firebase.google.com/) (Firestore & Authentication)
- **Data Visualization**: [Recharts](https://recharts.org/)
- **Icons**: [Lucide React](https://lucide.dev/)

## 🎨 UI Design Philosophy

MarketGazer features a professional **Dark Mode** interface with:
- **Primary Color**: A deep, sophisticated dark grey/blue (`#1a202c`).
- **Accent Colors**: "Forest Green" for growth and "Teal" for AI-driven insights.
- **Typography**: Space Grotesk for headlines and Inter for clean, readable body text.
- **Components**: Glassmorphism effects, subtle gradients, and highly interactive sidebar navigation.

## 🔒 Security

MarketGazer implements strict **Firebase Security Rules** to ensure that:
- User portfolios and preferences are private and only accessible by the owner.
- Market data (Stocks, Predictions, News) is globally readable but protected from unauthorized modifications.
- Authentication is handled securely via Firebase Auth.
